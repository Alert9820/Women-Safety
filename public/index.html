Guardian Angel

Your Safety is Our Priority

Guardian Angel is a comprehensive women safety app with advanced features to keep you protected 24/7, anywhere you go.

SOS

Press the SOS button in case of emergency. Your location will be shared with your trusted contacts immediately.

Key Features

Real-Time Tracking

Your loved ones can track your location in real-time through the app, ensuring your safety throughout your journey.

Nearby Safe Places

Find the nearest police stations, hospitals, and safe spots with directions on the interactive map.

Fake Call

Get a fake incoming call to help you escape uncomfortable situations by pretending to take an important call.

Loud Siren

Activate a loud alarm to attract attention and scare away potential attackers in emergency situations.

Voice Command

Use voice commands like "Help Me" to trigger alerts without touching your phone in critical situations.

Community Alerts

Women in your area can share safety warnings and report incidents to keep the community informed.

Advanced Protection Features

  • Volume Button SOS Trigger - Press volume button 3 times to send alert
  • Auto-Alert Mode - Detects abnormal movement and sends automatic alerts
  • Community Alert System - Share and receive safety warnings
  • Digital Guardian - Stay connected with loved ones for peace of mind
  • Web-Based SOS - Send alerts even without the app installed
  • Live Location Sharing - Share your journey in real-time
